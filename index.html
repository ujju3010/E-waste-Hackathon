About Us

We have created this website as a prototype for our 'Avinya-2024' hackathon at IIT Guwahati. Our team, CodeWarriorz, is tackling the problem of e-waste recycling in India.

E-waste recycling in India faces challenges primarily due to the informal sector, which contributes the most to recycling but often uses unsafe methods that cause harm to the workers and the environment.

E-Waste Collection

Offer convenient home pick-up services for customers and establish strategic take-back booths in public areas for easy drop-offs.

Efficient Transportation

Ensure efficient transportation to recycling centers using optimized routes and secure transportation practices to protect e-waste during transit.

Thorough Sorting and Processing

Carefully remove removable components like batteries and hard drives. Shred the remaining e-waste into smaller pieces and use optical sorting machines for precise differentiation.

Sustainable Recycling

Employ a variety of recycling methods, including rolling into wires and creating artistic products. Adhere to strict environmental regulations and standards throughout the recycling process.

Product Sales

Showcase recycled products in an attractive and user-friendly online store. Use eco-friendly packaging materials for sustainable product delivery.
